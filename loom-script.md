# Evergreen Loom Script - HUB Appliances

> Duration: 2-3 minutes
> Type: Cinematic Loom (screen recording with bespoke hook)
> Screen: Hub page open in browser

---

## Part 1: Hook (15 seconds) [BESPOKE]

"Hey Tomas, Samuel, Wilmer. This is Henryque from Zytrom. I spent some time researching HUB Appliances and I put together something I think you will want to see. Let me walk you through it."

## Part 2: Why You (15 seconds) [BESPOKE]

"I found HUB because I was looking at B2B appliance suppliers in South Florida that are growing fast but have not built a marketing system yet. You went from zero to a multi-million-dollar operation in under two years. That is impressive. But when I looked at how developers find you online, I noticed some gaps. So I built a few things."

## Part 3: Walk-Through (90-120 seconds) [SCREEN SHARE]

*Open hub page on screen. Scroll slowly through each section.*

"This is a page I built specifically for HUB Appliances. Everything here is based on real research into your business, your market, and your competition."

*Click open Research Snapshot accordion*

"First, I researched your website, your social presence, your competitive landscape, and the South Florida B2B appliance market. I found 5 specific gaps. The two biggest ones: you have zero social proof online. No Google reviews, no testimonials on your site, no case studies. For developers making decisions on 50 to 300 unit orders, that is a major trust barrier. Second, you have no content or thought leadership. Developers searching for 'bulk appliance supplier South Florida' or 'appliance packages for multi-family' cannot find you. Competitors who publish that content capture the lead before you enter the conversation."

*Click open Campaign Angles accordion*

"I wrote 5 campaign angles you could use in ads, emails, or social content. Each one targets a different pain point your clients deal with. For example, 'The $40K Delay' leads with the real financial cost of an unreliable appliance supplier."

*Click open Landing Page Preview accordion*

"Then I built you a complete landing page. This uses your exact brand identity. Your black and gold palette, your fonts, your positioning. It has a hero, a problem section, your solution, service tiers, a lead magnet, your about section, and FAQs. It is live right now."

*Pause briefly*

"I also wrote 3 ad scripts and a full VSL outline. Those are in here too."

## Part 4: Close (15-20 seconds) [BACK TO CAMERA]

"Everything on this page is yours. Whether we work together or not. If you want to talk about turning this into a full marketing system for HUB, or if you want help building a client acquisition engine that matches how fast you are growing, here is my calendar link. 30 minutes. No pitch. Just a conversation about growth."

*Point to calendar link on screen*

"Talk soon."

---

## Recording Notes

- Record the hook and "why you" parts looking at the camera. Keep it casual and confident.
- Switch to screen share for the walk-through. Move slowly. Let them see the details.
- Keep the total time under 3 minutes. People drop off after that.
- Do not read from a script. Use these points as a guide and speak naturally.
- Film in good lighting. Clean background. Professional but not stiff.
- Primary delivery channel: Instagram DM (@hub_appliances) with a short intro message and the Loom link. Secondary: email to info@hubappliances.com.
