FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does HUB Appliances serve?
We serve developers, contractors, and property managers across Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach, and Naples. Our warehouse is located in Medley, FL, and we deliver with our own fleet throughout these areas.
What size projects does HUB Appliances handle?
Everything from a single-unit renovation to 300+ unit developments. The process is the same: locked pricing, scheduled delivery, and a dedicated contact. Project size does not change how we operate.
Does HUB Appliances handle delivery and installation?
Yes. We deliver with our own fleet and coordinate installation with your GC or maintenance team. No third-party carriers. We control the schedule and the crew.
What happens if an appliance unit arrives damaged?
Because we warehouse locally and maintain inventory, we can replace damaged units within 48 hours rather than the 10-day industry standard. We inspect all units before they leave our facility.
Does HUB Appliances offer financing?
Yes. We offer Synchrony financing for clients who need payment flexibility. Contact us for details on available terms and qualification.
How do I get started with HUB Appliances?
Call 786-867-5405 or email info@hubappliances.com. Tell us about your project: unit count, timeline, and any specific appliance requirements. We will put together a package with locked pricing and a delivery schedule within 48 hours.
